Chicken

in 1817 baron von daron a walking machine that could help him get around the royal garden faster , two same size in-line wheels , the front one steerable , mounted in a frame upon which you straddled. the device was propelled by pushing your feet against the ground , thus rooling yourself and device forward in a sort of gliding walk.
